2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember preferences, and provide information to website owners.

  • Session cookies: temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent cookies: cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
  • First-party cookies: cookies set by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party cookies: cookies set by a domain other than the website you are visiting.
